Women in Business

In 2019, I co-founded an organization at the University of New Orleans with a classmate and friend of mine, Alexandra Heuer. With the goal of bridging the gender gap for leaders in business by giving women the tools to succeed, Women in Business started as a small idea that has now grown to a strong community on campus today. To help them expand their web presence, I used WordPress, PHP, and Custom Theming to create this website, which features a blog, a live Instagram feed connected to their account, and more!

Built with WordPress, PHP, Custom Theming, HTML, CSS, Adobe Photoshop, Responsive Web Design & Mobile-First Principles, Media Queries, Flexbox, Responsive Typography, and Standard Breakpoints

Hello! I'm Nina, a web developer and creative thinker in Memphis. I began my venture into tech as a graduate student in the midst of the pandemic, realizing that the more skills I had that could be applied remotely, the better. At first, I approached it with a sense of practicality, knowing coding skills were in high demand. But that mindset changed quickly as I began to realize I had found something that I really loved to do. After many coding courses and hundreds of hours of YouTube tutorials, I decided to pursue web development full-time.

I use my love for design and eye for detail to write clean, organized code and create beautiful, functional developments — always with effective communication in mind. When I'm not honing my tech skills, you can find me cooking, gardening, and exploring Memphis!
